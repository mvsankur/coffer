Q 1. Is registering for Coffer Wallet free?

It's absolutely free! You need not spend a penny to become an Coffer Wallet user. Also, there are no extra charges for adding money to the wallet, wallet to wallet money transfer or shopping at a merchant site.

Q 2. How do I protect my Coffer Wallet account?

DO NOT SHARE your Coffer Wallet Password with anyone. Still if you feel, your Coffer Wallet password has been compromised at any time, please write to us at care@Cofferwallet.com or simply SMS RESET to 9971888888. You will receive a new password on your registered mobile number, which you can change to your liking.
Please Note: We never ask for your password when you call us.

Q 3. Is it secure to use Coffer Wallet?

Coffer Wallet is a RBI approved mobile wallet secured using highest level of encryption. Every Coffer Wallet transaction is password protected and guarded behind our advanced encryption, so your mind is always at peace while making online payments. Each time you transact on Coffer Wallet, you are sent SMS and e-mail alerts so that you are up-to-date on all activities occurring through your Coffer Wallet. Also, with our Prepaid VISA card you can shop online without fear of revealing your account details at various merchant sites.

Q 4. Will I be informed when my Coffer Wallet gets credited or debited?

Yes of course. We will keep you informed about any transactions/activity that takes place on your Coffer Wallet. A confirmation SMS and/or an email notification (if email is registered with us) will be sent, whenever a transaction is processed from your wallet.

Q 5. What if I my mobile phone get lost/stolen?

Don't worry! No one will be able to access your Coffer Wallet without entering the Coffer Wallet password. That's why we recommend that you DO NOT STORE YOUR Coffer WALLET PASSWORD ANYWHERE ON YOUR PHONE. However, for security reasons, if your phone is lost/stolen, inform us immediately by emailing us at care@Cofferwallet.com or calling our Customer Care at 1246-688-333, following which we will block your wallet for further safeguard.
